## Schedule RC-M — Memoranda

### 1. Extensions of Credit to Insiders
- **1.a.** Aggregate amount of all extensions of credit to all executive officers, directors, principal shareholders, and their related interests (RCON 6164)
- **1.b.** Number of executive officers, directors, and principal shareholders to whom extensions of credit equal or exceed the lesser of $500,000 or 5 percent of total capital (Number, RCON 6165)

### 2. Intangible Assets
- **2.a.** Mortgage servicing assets (RCON 3164)
  - (1) Estimated fair value of mortgage servicing assets (RCON A590)
- **2.b.** Goodwill (RCON 3163)
- **2.c.** All other intangible assets (RCON JF76)
- **2.d.** Total (sum of 2.a, 2.b, and 2.c) (must equal Schedule RC, item 10) (RCON 2143)

### 3. Other Real Estate Owned
- **3.a.** Construction, land development, and other land (RCON 5508)
- **3.b.** Farmland (RCON 5509)
- **3.c.** 1-4 family residential properties (RCON 5510)
- **3.d.** Multifamily (5 or more) residential properties (RCON 5511)
- **3.e.** Nonfarm nonresidential properties (RCON 5512)
- **3.f.** Total (sum of 3.a through 3.e) (must equal Schedule RC, item 7) (RCON 2150)

### 4. Cost of Equity Securities with Readily Determinable Fair Values Not Held for Trading (RCON JA29)
To be completed only by insured state banks approved by the FDIC to hold grandfathered equity investments.

### 5. Other Borrowed Money (RCON 3190 = total)

**5.a. Federal Home Loan Bank Advances:**
- (1) Advances with a remaining maturity or next repricing date of:
  - (a) One year or less (RCON F055)

  - (c) Over three years through five years (RCON F057)
  - (d) Over five years (RCON F058)
- (2) Advances with a REMAINING MATURITY of one year or less (RCON 2651)
- (3) Structured advances (RCON F059)

**5.b. Other Borrowings:**
- (1) Other borrowings with a remaining maturity or next repricing date of:
  - (a) One year or less (RCON F060)
  - (b) Over one year through three years (RCON F061)
  - (c) Over three years through five years (RCON F062)
  - (d) Over five years (RCON F063)
- (2) Other borrowings with a REMAINING MATURITY of one year or less (RCON B571)

**5.c.** Total (sum of items 5.a.(1)(a)-(d) and items 5.b.(1)(a)-(d)) (must equal Schedule RC, item 16) (RCON 3190)
