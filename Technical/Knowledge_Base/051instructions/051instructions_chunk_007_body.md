### Reporting Frequency Thresholds

**Total fiduciary assets > $1 billion** (item 10, sum of columns A and B, as of preceding December 31) OR gross fiduciary income > 10% of revenue:
- Items 4-22 and Memorandum item 3: quarterly
- Items 23-26: annually (December report)
- Memorandum items 1, 2, and 4: annually (December report)

**Total fiduciary assets > $250 million but <= $1 billion** (not meeting income test):
- Items 4-22 and Memorandum item 3: semiannually (June and December)
- Items 23-26: annually (December report)
- Memorandum items 1, 2, and 4: annually (December report)

**Total fiduciary assets <= $250 million** (not meeting income test):
- Items 4-13: annually (December report)
- Memorandum items 1-3: annually (December report)
- If fiduciary assets > $100 million but <= $250 million: also Memorandum item 4 annually (December)

## Optional Narrative Statement (Page 66)

The management of the reporting bank may submit a brief narrative statement on the amounts reported in the Consolidated Reports of Condition and Income.

### Key Rules
- Statement will be made available to the public along with publicly available Call Report data
- Maximum length: 100 words / 750 characters (including punctuation, indentation, and spacing)
- Statements exceeding 750 characters will be truncated without notice
- Must NOT contain: names or identifications of individual bank customers, references to confidential items (Schedule RI-E item 2.g; Schedule RC-C Part I Memorandum items 17.a and 17.b), or other information the bank does not wish to make public
- The statement will NOT be edited or screened by supervisory agencies for accuracy or relevance
- If material changes are later submitted to the reported data, the existing narrative statement will be deleted; the bank may replace it

### Codes
- **RCON 6979**: Comments indicator (Yes/No)
- **TEXT 6980**: Bank Management Statement text field

Banks choosing not to make a statement should check "No comment" and make no entries in the text space.
