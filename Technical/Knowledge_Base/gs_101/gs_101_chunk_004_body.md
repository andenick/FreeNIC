# FFIEC 101 Chunk 4 -- Schedules L, M, N, O

## Schedule L -- Retail Exposure: Residential Mortgage -- Closed-End Junior Lien Exposures (Pages L-1 and L-2)

**Effective Date**: 10/2022 (main table), 03/2014 (memoranda)

### Structure

Schedule L has the same 16-column structure as Schedule K (Columns A through P):

- Column A: Weighted-Average PD (Percentage, rounded to 2 decimal places)
- Column B: Number of Exposures (Number)
- Column C: Total Balance Sheet Amount (Amount)
- Column D: Total Undrawn Amount (Amount)
- Column E: EAD (Amount)
- Column F: Weighted-Average Age (Months, rounded to 2 decimal places)
- Column G: Weighted-Average LGD (Percentage, rounded to 2 decimal places)
- Column H: Risk-Weighted Assets (Amount) -- not calculated from previous column entries
- Column I: Expected Credit Loss (Amount)
- Column J: LTV Less Than 70% (Amount)
- Column K: LTV At Least 70% but Less Than 80% (Amount)
- Column L: LTV At Least 80% but Less Than 90% (Amount)
- Column M: LTV At Least 90% but Less Than 100% (Amount)
- Column N: LTV Greater than or Equal to 100% (Amount)
- Column O: Weighted-Average Bureau Score (Number, rounded to 1 decimal place; item 16 rounded to nearest whole number)
- Column P: EAD of Accounts with Updated LTV (Amount)

### LTV Notes

LTV values should be calculated by combining junior liens applicable to amounts on this schedule with prior lien amounts. Where LTV information is available for all accounts, the sum of EADs reported in Columns J through N for a given PD range should equal the amount reported in Column E for that same PD range. Otherwise, the sum will be less.

### PD Ranges (Items 1-16)

Same 15 PD ranges as Schedule K (0.00 to <0.05 through 100.00 Default, plus Total).

### Memoranda

- **M.1**: Risk-weighted assets associated with non-material portfolios not included above (MDRM: AALX J036)
- **M.2**: Credit scores shown in Column O are from which credit scoring system(s)? (MDRM: AALX J041)

## Schedule N -- Retail Exposure: Qualifying Revolving Exposures (Pages N-1 and N-2)

**Effective Date**: 10/2022 (main table), 03/2014 (memoranda)

### Structure

Schedule N has 10 columns (A through J):

- Column A: Weighted-Average PD (Percentage, rounded to 2 decimal places)
- Column B: Number of Exposures (Number)
- Column C: Total Balance Sheet Amount (Amount)
- Column D: Total Undrawn Amount (Amount)
- Column E: EAD (Amount)
- Column F: EAD of Accounts Less Than Two Years Old (Amount)
- Column G: Weighted-Average LGD (Percentage, rounded to 2 decimal places)
- Column H: Risk-Weighted Assets (Amount) -- not calculated from previous column entries
- Column I: Expected Credit Loss (Amount)
- Column J: Weighted-Average Bureau Score (Number, rounded to 1 decimal place; item 16 rounded to nearest whole number)

### PD Ranges (Items 1-16)

1. 0.00 to < 0.50
2. 0.50 to < 1.00
3. 1.00 to < 1.50
4. 1.50 to < 2.00
5. 2.00 to < 2.50
6. 2.50 to < 3.00
7. 3.00 to < 3.50
8. 3.50 to < 4.00
9. 4.00 to < 5.00
10. 5.00 to < 6.00
11. 6.00 to < 7.00
12. 7.00 to < 8.00
13. 8.00 to < 10.00
14. 10.00 to < 100
15. 100.00 (default)
16. Total (calculated, except for Column J)

### Memoranda

- **M.1**: Risk-weighted assets associated with non-material portfolios not included above (MDRM: AANX J036)
- **M.2**: Credit scores shown in Column J are from which credit scoring system(s)? (MDRM: AANX J041)

### Notes

1. Cells in line 16 are calculated, except for Column J.
2. Report weighted averages in Columns A and G rounded to two decimal places.
3. Risk-weighted assets are not calculated from previous column entries.
4. Report weighted averages in Column J rounded to one decimal place, except for item 16, which should be rounded to the nearest whole number.
