Provenance

The bank_failures table is real FDIC BankFind failure/assistance records (public), harmonized in the FreeNIC pipeline alongside the academic Robin failing-banks compilation. Monetary fields (total_assets, total_deposits, estimated_loss) are in US-dollar thousands ($000s) as published by FDIC; nulls (e.g. some estimated-loss values) are shown as —, never invented.
