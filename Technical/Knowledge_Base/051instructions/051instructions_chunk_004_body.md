# FFIEC 051 — Chunk 004: Schedule RC-K, RC-L, RC-M, RC-N, RC-O

## Schedule RC-K — Quarterly Averages

Banks have the option of reporting either:
1. An average of **daily** figures for the quarter, or
2. An average of **weekly** figures (i.e., the Wednesday of each week of the quarter)

Quarterly averages for all debt securities should be based on amortized cost. Quarterly averages for equity securities with readily determinable fair values should be based on fair value.

## Schedule RC-N — Past Due and Nonaccrual Loans, Leases, and Other Assets

Three-column format:
- Column A: Past due 30 through 89 days and still accruing
- Column B: Past due 90 days or more and still accruing
- Column C: Nonaccrual

Reports amounts for each loan/lease category matching Schedule RC-C Part I categories, plus debt securities and other assets.

### Items 11-12: Government-Guaranteed and FDIC Loss-Sharing Covered Loans

- Item 11: Loans wholly or partially guaranteed by the U.S. Government (excluding FDIC loss-sharing)
- Item 11.a.: Guaranteed portion excluding rebooked GNMA loans
- Item 11.b.: Rebooked GNMA loans repurchased or eligible for repurchase
- Item 12: Portion covered by FDIC loss-sharing agreements

### Memoranda

- M.1.: Loan modifications to borrowers experiencing financial difficulty (by category, semiannual for detail, quarterly for total)
- M.2.: Loans to finance commercial RE, construction, and land development (not secured by RE)
- M.4.: Loans to finance agricultural production (conditional)
- M.5.: Loans and leases held for sale (semiannual)
- M.7.: Additions to nonaccrual assets during the previous six months (semiannual)
- M.8.: Nonaccrual assets sold during the previous six months (semiannual)
- M.9.: Loans to nondepository financial institutions
