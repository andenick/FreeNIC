## Schedule RC-C — Loans and Lease Financing Receivables

### Part I. Loans and Leases

**General Instructions:**
- Do not deduct the allowance for credit losses on loans and leases or the allocated transfer risk reserve from amounts reported in this schedule.
- Report (1) loans and leases held for sale at the lower of cost or fair value, (2) loans and leases held for investment, net of unearned income, and (3) loans and leases accounted for at fair value under a fair value option.
- Exclude assets held for trading and commercial paper.

**Column Structure:**
- Column A: To be completed by banks with $300 million or more in total assets (based on June 30, 2024 Report of Condition)
- Column B: To be completed by all banks

#### Item 1. Loans Secured by Real Estate

**1.a. Construction, land development, and other land loans:**
- (1) 1-4 family residential construction loans (RCON F158)
- (2) Other construction loans and all land development and other land loans (RCON F159)

**1.b.** Secured by farmland (including farm residential and other improvements) (RCON 1420)

**1.c. Secured by 1-4 family residential properties:**
- (1) Revolving, open-end loans secured by 1-4 family residential properties and extended under lines of credit (RCON 1797)
- (2) Closed-end loans secured by 1-4 family residential properties:
  - (a) Secured by first liens (RCON 5367)
  - (b) Secured by junior liens (RCON 5368)

**1.d.** Secured by multifamily (5 or more) residential properties (RCON 1460)

**1.e. Secured by nonfarm nonresidential properties:**
- (1) Loans secured by owner-occupied nonfarm nonresidential properties (RCON F160)
- (2) Loans secured by other nonfarm nonresidential properties (RCON F161)

#### Item 2. Loans to Depository Institutions and Acceptances of Other Banks (RCON 1288)
- **2.a.** To commercial banks in the U.S. (RCON B531)
- **2.b.** To other depository institutions in the U.S. (RCON B534)
- **2.c.** To banks in foreign countries (RCON B535)

#### Item 3. Loans to Finance Agricultural Production and Other Loans to Farmers (RCON 1590)

#### Item 4. Commercial and Industrial Loans (RCON 1766)
- **4.a.** To U.S. addressees (domicile) (RCON 1763)
- **4.b.** To non-U.S. addressees (domicile) (RCON 1764)

#### Item 5. Not Applicable

#### Item 6. Loans to Individuals for Household, Family, and Other Personal Expenditures (Consumer Loans) (includes purchased paper)
- **6.a.** Credit cards (RCON B538)
- **6.b.** Other revolving credit plans (RCON B539)
- **6.c.** Automobile loans (RCON K137)
- **6.d.** Other consumer loans (includes single payment and installment, loans other than automobile loans, and all student loans) (RCON K207)

#### Item 7. Not Applicable

#### Item 8. Obligations (Other Than Securities and Leases) of States and Political Subdivisions in the U.S. (RCON 2107)

- **10.a.** Leases to individuals for household, family, and other personal expenditures (i.e., consumer leases) (RCON F162)
- **10.b.** All other leases (RCON F163)

Sum of items 1 through 10 minus item 11. Must equal Schedule RC, sum of items 4.a and 4.b.
