# FR Y-14M Instructions - Chunk 019
## Schedule D.2 Portfolio Level Table (concluded): Line Items 28-46

This chunk concludes the Schedule D (Domestic Credit Card) D.2 Portfolio Level Table, covering Line Items 28 through 46. This is the final chunk of the FR Y-14M Instructions document.

### Line Item 28: Interest Expense (MDRM N158)
Report the total interest expenses accrued for the month to fund credit card receivables.

### Line Item 29: Total Non-Interest Expense (MDRM N159)
Report the total non-interest expenses.

### Line Item 30: Total Non-Interest Expense - Interchange Expense (MDRM N160)
Report the interchange expense fees paid to the card associations.

### Line Item 31: Total Non-Interest Expense - Rewards/Rebates Expense (MDRM N161)
Report the rewards/rebates expenses associated with reward and rebate programs for credit cards.

### Line Item 32: Total Non-Interest Expense - Collections Expense (MDRM N162)
Report costs incurred to collect problem credits. Include the total collection cost for delinquent, recovery, and bankrupt accounts.

### Line Item 33: Total Non-Interest Expense - Fraud Expense (Effective for submission of June 2013 data) (MDRM N163)
Report losses incurred as a result of fraudulent activity.

### Line Item 34: Total Non-Interest Expense - All Other Expenses (MDRM N164)
Report all other operating and other expenses associated with card operations. This includes servicing, cardholder billing, processing interchange, processing payments, card issuing, authorizations, card administration and outside services/outsourcing expenses, etc.

### Line Item 35: Interest Income (MDRM N165)
Report total interest accrued and billed on credit card receivables for the month, less any interest waived or reversed as uncollectible or any amount added to a contra-asset account for uncollectible interest that the bank maintains and reports separately from the allowance for credit losses on loans and leases. Do not include fee income in this category.

### Line Item 36: Fee Income (MDRM N166)
Report total fees assessed on credit card accounts during the month, less any fee waived or reversed as uncollectible or any amount added to a contra-asset account for uncollectible fees that the bank maintains and reports separately from the allowance for credit losses on loans and leases. Interchange, annual and debt suspension/cancellation fees should not be reported here.

### Line Item 37: Fee Income - Late Fee Income (MDRM N167)
Report net fees assessed for late or nonpayment accounts.

### Line Item 38: Fee Income - Over Limit Fee Income (MDRM N168)
Report fees assessed for accounts going over established credit limits.

### Line Item 39: Fee Income - Balance Transfer Fee (MDRM N169)
Report fees assessed for transferring balances from another source outside the bank, such as another financial institution's credit card to a credit card at this bank.

### Line Item 40: Fee Income - Convenience Check Fee (MDRM N170)
Report fees assessed for the use of checks provided to assess available credit on the account.

### Line Item 41: Fee Income - Cash Advance Fee (Effective for submission of June 2013 data) (MDRM N171)
Report fees assessed for cash advances on the account.

### Line Item 42: Fee Income - NSF Fee (MDRM N172)
Report fees assessed for payments returned unpaid.

### Line Item 43: Fee Income - Other Fee Income (MDRM N173)
Report other activity-based fees assessed on an account, such as foreign exchange conversion fees, not reported in Line items 37-42 but included in Line item 36.

### Line Item 44: Interchange Income (MDRM N174)
Report interchange fees received from the card associations.

### Line Item 45: All Other Non-Interest Income (MDRM N175)
Report any other non-interest income as a part of total income that is not reported in line items 35, 36 (summation of 37-43), and 44, including annual fees, debt suspension/cancellation product fees, etc., associated with card operations.

### Line Item 46: Taxes (MDRM N176)
Report tax expenses applicable to credit card income.
