# FFIEC 051 — Chunk 003: Schedule RC-C, RC-E, RC-F, RC-G

## Schedule RC-C — Loans and Lease Financing Receivables

### Part I. Loans and Leases

Do not deduct the allowance for credit losses on loans and leases or the allocated transfer risk reserve from amounts reported in this schedule. Report:
1. Loans and leases held for sale at the lower of cost or fair value
2. Loans and leases held for investment, net of unearned income
3. Loans and leases accounted for at fair value under a fair value option

Exclude assets held for trading and commercial paper.

### Part I — Memoranda

#### M.1. Loan Modifications to Borrowers Experiencing Financial Difficulty (in compliance with modified terms)

Reported semiannually in June and December (items 1.a.(1) through 1.f.(5)); item 1.g is quarterly. Included in Schedule RC-C Part I and not reported as past due or nonaccrual in Schedule RC-N Memo item 1.

#### M.2. Maturity and Repricing Data for Loans and Leases

Report fixed-rate loans by remaining maturity and floating-rate loans by next repricing date.

- **M.2.a.** Closed-end loans secured by first liens on 1-4 family residential properties: six time buckets from 3 months or less through over 15 years
- **M.2.b.** All other loans and leases excluding 1-4 family first lien closed-end: six time buckets
- **M.2.c.** Loans and leases with remaining maturity of one year or less

#### M.3. Loans to Finance Commercial Real Estate, Construction, and Land Development (not secured by real estate)

Included in Schedule RC-C, Part I, items 4 and 9. Exclude loans secured by real estate in items 1.a through 1.e.

#### M.4. Adjustable-Rate Closed-End Loans Secured by First Liens on 1-4 Family Residential Properties

### Part II. Loans to Small Businesses and Small Farms

Completed **semiannually in the June and December reports only**.

Report number and amount currently outstanding as of the report date of:
- Business loans with original amounts of $1,000,000 or less
- Farm loans with original amounts of $500,000 or less

**Original amount determination**:
1. For lines of credit/commitments: size of the line when most recently approved, extended, or renewed (or amount outstanding if larger)
2. For participations/syndications: entire amount of the credit originated by lead lender
3. For all other loans: total amount at origination or amount currently outstanding, whichever is larger

#### Loans to Small Businesses (Items 1-4)

- Item 1: Yes/No — whether all/substantially all nonfarm nonresidential property loans and C&I loans have original amounts of $100,000 or less
- Items 2.a-2.b: Total number of loans (if Yes to item 1)
- Items 3.a-3.c: Nonfarm nonresidential property loans by original amount band ($100K or less / $100K-$250K / $250K-$1M)
- Items 4.a-4.c: C&I loans by original amount band ($100K or less / $100K-$250K / $250K-$1M)

#### Agricultural Loans to Small Farms (Items 5-8)

- Item 5: Yes/No — whether all/substantially all farmland loans and ag production loans have original amounts of $100,000 or less
- Items 6.a-6.b: Total number of loans (if Yes to item 5)
- Items 7.a-7.c: Farmland loans by original amount band ($100K or less / $100K-$250K / $250K-$500K)
- Items 8.a-8.c: Ag production loans by original amount band ($100K or less / $100K-$250K / $250K-$500K)
