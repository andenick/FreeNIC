## Pages 46-48 — Schedule HC-Q: Assets and Liabilities Measured at Fair Value on a Recurring Basis

Schedule HC-Q is to be completed by all holding companies with $5 billion or more in total assets that:
(1) Have elected to report financial instruments or servicing assets and liabilities at fair value under a fair value option, or
(2) Are required to complete Schedule HC-D, Trading Assets and Liabilities.

Five-column format:
- Column A: Total Fair Value Reported on Schedule HC
- Column B: LESS: Amounts Netted in the Determination of Total Fair Value
- Column C: Level 1 Fair Value Measurements
- Column D: Level 2 Fair Value Measurements
- Column E: Level 3 Fair Value Measurements

### Assets (items 1-7):
- 1. Available-for-sale debt and equity securities with RDFV not held for trading
- 2. Federal funds sold and securities purchased under agreements to resell
- 3. Loans and leases held for sale
- 4. Loans and leases held for investment
- 5.a. Trading assets: Derivative assets
- 5.b. Trading assets: Other trading assets
- 5.b.(1). Nontrading securities at fair value
- 6. All other assets
- 7. Total assets measured at fair value on a recurring basis

### Liabilities (items 8-14):
- 8. Deposits
- 9. Federal funds purchased and securities sold under agreements to repurchase
- 10.a. Trading liabilities: Derivative liabilities
- 10.b. Trading liabilities: Other trading liabilities
- 11. Other borrowed money
- 12. Subordinated notes and debentures
- 13. All other liabilities
- 14. Total liabilities measured at fair value on a recurring basis

### Memoranda:
- M.1. All other assets itemization (MSAs, nontrading derivative assets, and custom text items)
- M.2. All other liabilities itemization (loan commitments, nontrading derivative liabilities, and custom text items)
- M.3. Loans measured at fair value (by loan type)
- M.4. Unpaid principal balances of loans measured at fair value (by loan type)
