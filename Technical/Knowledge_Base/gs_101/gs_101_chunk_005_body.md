## Schedule P -- Securitization Exposures (Page P-1)

**Effective Date**: 03/2014

### Structure

Schedule P has 6 columns split into two groups:

**Securitizations (excluding resecuritizations):**
- Column A: Exposure Amount (Amount)
- Column B: Risk-Weighted Assets (Amount)
- Column C: Deduction (Amount)

**Resecuritizations:**
- Column D: Exposure Amount (Amount)
- Column E: Risk-Weighted Assets (Amount)
- Column F: Deduction (Amount)

### Items

1. Exposures subject to the supervisory formula approach
   - Securitizations: AAPP P932 (Exposure), AAPP P933 (RWA)
   - Resecuritizations: AAPP P934 (Exposure), AAPP P935 (RWA)

2. Exposures subject to the simplified supervisory formula approach
   - Securitizations: AAPP P936 (Exposure), AAPP P937 (RWA)
   - Resecuritizations: AAPP P938 (Exposure), AAPP P939 (RWA)

3. Exposures subject to 1,250 percent risk weight
   - Securitizations: AAPP P940 (Exposure), AAPP P941 (RWA)
   - Resecuritizations: AAPP P942 (Exposure), AAPP P943 (RWA)

4. Exposures subject to deduction
   - Securitizations: AAPP P944 (Deduction)
   - Resecuritizations: AAPP P945 (Deduction)

## Schedule Q -- Cleared Transactions (Page Q-1)

**Effective Date**: 03/2014

### Structure

Schedule Q has 4 columns:

- Column A: Exposure amount with QCCP qualifying for 2% risk weight (Amount)
- Column B: Exposure amount not qualifying for 2% risk weight (Amount)
- Column C: Exposure amount for default fund contributions (Amount)
- Column D: Risk-Weighted Assets (Amount)

QCCP = Qualifying Central Counterparty

### Items

**Clearing Member Client Bank:**
1. Derivative contracts or netting sets of derivative contracts (MDRM: AAQQ P950, P951, P952)
2. Repo-style transactions (MDRM: AAQQ P953, P954, P955)

**Clearing Member Bank:**
3. Derivative contracts or netting sets of derivative contracts (MDRM: AAQQ P956, P957, P958)
4. Repo-style transactions (MDRM: AAQQ P959, P960, P961)

5. Default fund contributions to non-QCCP (MDRM: AAQQ P962, P963)
6. Default fund contributions to QCCP (MDRM: AAQQ P964, P965)

**Total:**
7. Total clearing member exposures and risk-weighted assets (MDRM: AAQQ P966, P967, P968, P969)

## Schedule R -- Equity Exposures (Pages R-1 and R-2)

**Effective Date**: 03/2018 (R-1), 03/2014 (R-2)

### Structure

Schedule R has 6 columns organized across three approaches:

**Simple Risk Weight Approach:**
- Column A: Exposure (Amount)
- Column B: Risk-Weighted Assets (Amount)

**Full Internal Models Approach:**
- Column C: Exposure (Amount)
- Column D: Risk-Weighted Assets (Amount)

**Publicly Traded Internal Models Approach:**
- Column E: Exposure (Amount)
- Column F: Risk-Weighted Assets (Amount)

### Items (Page R-1)

1. Total equity exposures -- Columns A, C, E only (MDRM: AARA J053, AARC J053, AARE J053)
2. 0% risk weight (MDRM suffix J054, all 6 columns)
3. 20% risk weight (MDRM suffix J055, all 6 columns)
4. Community development equity exposures -- 100% risk weight (MDRM suffix J056, all 6 columns)

**Simple Risk Weight Approach (SRWA):**
5. Effective portion of hedge pairs -- 100% (Columns A, B only; MDRM suffix J057)
6. Non-significant equity exposures -- 100% (Columns A, B only; MDRM suffix J058)
7. Significant investments in unconsolidated financial institutions -- 250% (Columns A, B only; MDRM suffix P970)
8. Publicly traded equity exposures under SRWA -- 300% (Columns A, B only; MDRM suffix J059)
9. Non-publicly traded equity exposures under SRWA -- 400% (Columns A, B and E, F; MDRM suffix J060)
10. 600% risk-weight equity exposures under SRWA -- 600% (Columns A, B and E, F; MDRM suffix J061)
11. Total RWA under the SRWA (sum column B, lines 2 through 10) (Column B only; MDRM: AARB J062)

**Equity Exposures to Investment Funds:**
12. Full look-through approach (all 6 columns; MDRM suffix J063)
13. Simple modified look-through approach (all 6 columns; MDRM suffix J064)
14. Alternative modified look-through approach (all 6 columns; MDRM suffix J065)

### Items (Page R-2)

15. Total RWA for investment funds (sum columns B, D, and F, lines 12 through 14) (MDRM: AARB J067, AARD J067, AARF J067)
16. Total: SRWA (column B, lines 11 and 15) (MDRM: AARB J068)

**Full Internal Models Approach (Full IMA):**
17. Estimate of potential losses on equity exposures -- 12.5 multiplier (Columns C, D; MDRM: AARC J069, AARD J069)

**Floors (Full IMA):**
18. Publicly traded -- 200% (Columns C, D; MDRM: AARC J070, AARD J070)
19. Non-publicly traded -- 300% (Columns C, D; MDRM: AARC J071, AARD J071)
20. RWA floors (add from column D, lines 18 and 19) (MDRM: AARD J072)
21. Total RWA -- Full IMA (larger of column D, lines 17 and 20) (MDRM: AARD J073)
22. Total: Full IMA (add from column D lines 3, 4, 15, and 21) (MDRM: AARD J074)

**Publicly Traded Internal Models Approach (Partial IMA):**

**Floors (Partial IMA):**
