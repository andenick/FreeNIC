### Reporting Thresholds

**Institutions with total fiduciary assets (item 10, sum of columns A and B) > $250 million** (as of preceding December 31) OR with gross fiduciary and related services income > 10% of revenue for the preceding calendar year must complete:
- Items 4-22 and Memorandum item 3 quarterly
- Items 23-26 annually with the December report
- Memorandum items 1, 2, and 4 annually with the December report

**Institutions with total fiduciary assets <= $250 million** that do not meet the fiduciary income test must complete:
- Items 4-13 annually with the December report
- Memorandum items 1-3 annually with the December report
- Institutions with total fiduciary assets > $100M but <= $250M must also complete Memorandum item 4 annually with December report

## Optional Narrative Statement (Page 86)

The management of the reporting bank may submit a brief narrative statement on the amounts reported in the Consolidated Reports of Condition and Income. This optional statement will be made available to the public.

**Confidential items** (will NOT be made available to the public on an individual institution basis):
- Schedule RI-E, item 2.g
- Schedule RC-C, Part I, Memorandum items 17.a and 17.b
- Schedule RC-O, Memorandum items 6 through 9, 14, 15, and 18
- Schedule RC-P, items 7.a and 7.b

**Statement requirements**:
- Must not contain names or other identifications of individual bank customers
- Must not reference confidential items listed above
- Must not exceed 100 words / 750 characters (including punctuation, indentation, and standard spacing)
- Statements exceeding 750 characters will be truncated without notice
- Must be accurate and not misleading
- Will NOT be edited or screened by supervisory agencies for accuracy or relevance
