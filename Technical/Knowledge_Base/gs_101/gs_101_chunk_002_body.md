## Schedule C -- Wholesale Exposure: Corporate (Pages C-1 and C-2)

**Effective Date**: 10/2022 (main table), 03/2016 (memoranda)

### Structure

Schedule C reports wholesale corporate exposures by PD range with 12 columns:

- Column A: Weighted-Average Obligor PD (Percentage, rounded to 2 decimal places)
- Column B: Number of Obligors (Number)
- Column C: Balance Sheet Amount (Amount)
- Column D: Total Undrawn Amount (Amount)
- Column E: EAD (Amount)
- Column F: Weighted-Average Effective Maturity (Years, rounded to 2 decimal places)
- Column G: Weighted-Average LGD before Consideration of Eligible Guarantees and Credit Derivatives (Percentage, rounded to 2 decimal places)
- Column H: Weighted-Average LGD after Consideration of Credit Risk Mitigants (Percentage, rounded to 2 decimal places)
- Column I: Effect of PD Substitution and LGD Adjustment Approaches on RWA (Amount)
- Column J: Effect of Double Default Treatment on RWA (Amount)
- Column K: Risk-Weighted Assets (Amount) -- not calculated from previous column entries
- Column L: Expected Credit Loss (Amount)

### PD Ranges (Items 1-13)

1. 0.00 to < 0.15
2. 0.15 to < 0.25
3. 0.25 to < 0.35
4. 0.35 to < 0.50
5. 0.50 to < 0.75
6. 0.75 to < 1.35
7. 1.35 to < 2.50
8. 2.50 to < 5.50
9. 5.50 to < 10.00
10. 10.00 to < 20.00
11. 20.00 to < 100
12. 100.00 (default) -- Column A is fixed at 100.00
13. Total -- Cells in line 13 are calculated

### Memoranda

- **M.1**: Risk-weighted assets associated with non-material portfolios not included above (MDRM: AACX J036)
- **M.2**: Regulated financial institutions -- Exposures subject to a wholesale correlation factor multiplier of 1.25 (all 12 columns, MDRM suffix P929)
- **M.3**: Unregulated financial institutions -- Exposures subject to a wholesale correlation factor multiplier of 1.25 (all 12 columns, MDRM suffix P930)
